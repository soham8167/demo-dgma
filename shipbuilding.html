Cochin Shipyard Limited

📍 Kochi, Kerala • Commercial & Defense

110,000 DWT

2 Major Docks

1972

Cochin Shipyard Limited (CSL) is the premier ship building and maintenance facility in India. Built with Japanese assistance, CSL can build ships up to 110,000 DWT and repair ships up to 125,000 DWT. It has constructed India's first indigenous aircraft carrier INS Vikrant.
